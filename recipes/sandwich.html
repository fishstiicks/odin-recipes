Sandwich

Description

I'm sure you've heard of the debate: what is a sandwich? Is a hotdog a sandwich? Today, we are defining a sandwich as anything between two slices of bread.

Just like grandma used to make.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Anything else

Steps

  1. Take out two slices/loaves/whatever bread you have
  2. Put the other ingreding between them
  3. Vertical stack
  4. Appreciate your sandwich
